    • What is Rarity

    Rarity is a brand new NFT marketplace powered by the Cardano blockchain where many digital artists can create breathe taking art and mint them into Non fungible tokens within seconds. In addition to the seemless NFT creation, Rarity provides a marketplace for digital art enthusiasts to trade NFTs in a fast, reliable, and safe manner with little to no extra fees.

    Rarity makes use of the Cardano blockchain to keep track of all the tokens minted using its services. Each Non-Fungible token created on Rarity is stored as a native token on the Cardano ledger, which is different than the traditional NFTs on other marketplaces that make use of Smart Contracts for minting. This native minting makes it extremely fast and cheap, where huge gas fees for minting NFTs using smart contracts is not required anymore in order to transfer NFTs. In addition, cardano plutus smart contracts are still put in use to make sure that all transactions and trades are automated and instantaneous.

    Rarity's vision is to have a reliable marketplace that does not mess up its user's experience regarding minting, buying, and selling NFTs. We want to ensure that each time a user wants to buy an NFT and they have sufficient funds, then the NFT should go to them incase they were the first to get to that Buy button. We also ensure that no failing transactions would cost the users any money, since their experience and hard earned money are of extreme importance to us. Lastly, we promise a fair market that is empty from insider trading of NFT and prevent the use of bots to buy up certain awaited drops. Our most important goal is to have a fair market where everyone is treated equaly and each and every person has the same equal chances of making it big.

    Rarity is powered by Cardano and its use of a functional language called Haskell as a base. Rarity does not ask for the user to enter any private information or seed to a wallet and ensures that all private data of the user are not stored on its servers. Rarity does not handel the cryptocurrencies of the user and requires the user to connect his wallet to Rarity which enables the transactions without Rarity being a third party or involved in any means. This makes the transactions secure due to the nature of cryptocurrencies in general and how it is extremely difficult to hack and modify transcations.

    Our team is devoted once again to make sure that the market is fair and that all users are treated equaly. This is the reason why make it a strict policy that all Rarity employees not trade on Rarity to ensure that no one is having insider sensitive information about certain NFT drops that are of great value and to aquire them at low prics before they hit the main marketplace of Rarity. This decision also helps ensure that no employee that has information about what drops will be featured on the home page in future times, would buy that drop since this would most certainly increase its value.

    Our users can take advantage of many perks such as price indicators to check whether the nft is undervalued or overvalued. It also shows the overall trends in the markets as well as many useful infromation to make trading decisions base on. In addition, our users will be opted in several free limited edition airdrops of tokens which may hold a lot of value in the future. Our platform and logic will be made open source and all our users will get the chance to vote on what features they want implemented and what features would they rather have changed or even removed.

    The minting fees are almost close to zero due to making use of the Cardano technology which is extremely advanced and uses the state of the art cryptography techniques. Buying and selling also takes little fees as much as 0.22 ADA which is not even close to a single US dollar. Rarity would like to keep its business model more focused on getting funds from donations to keep the wheel going and not bombard the users with extra fees, this is why we decided to only keep the fees of using our services at the tiny rate of 0.05% of each transaction made and did not add any fees for minting NFTs.
